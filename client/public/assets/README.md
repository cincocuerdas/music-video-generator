# Cinematic Loader Video

Place your cinematic loading animation here as `cinematic-loader.mp4`.

## Video Specifications

- **Duration:** 5-7 seconds (seamless loop)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Size:** < 5MB recommended

## Prompt for AI Video Generation

Use this prompt with Runway, Pika, Kling, or similar:

```
A timeless cinematic scene depicting the act of filmmaking itself.

A vintage early-20th-century film camera, crafted from dark metal and worn brass, mounted on a sturdy tripod. The camera faces forward, aimed directly at a static album cover positioned as the subject being filmed.

A human hand slowly and rhythmically turns the camera's mechanical side crank. The motion is continuous, deliberate, and hypnotic — not rushed, not exaggerated. This motion is the heart of the scene.

The album cover remains perfectly still, centered, sharp, and dignified, as if posing for history.

Lighting is soft and cinematic, warm tungsten tones with gentle falloff. Subtle shadows breathe life into the metal surfaces. Fine dust particles float through the air, barely visible, catching the light like time itself.

The camera exhibits minimal physical presence: a slight mechanical sway, a nearly imperceptible push-in, as if the operator is breathing. Nothing feels digital. Everything feels handcrafted.

Texture includes fine film grain, mild vignetting, and analog imperfections that suggest celluloid, not simulation.

The background is dark, neutral, and uncluttered — a stage for the ritual of creation.

No text. No logos. No modern elements. No screens.

Mood: reverent, calm, artistic, inevitable.

Duration: seamless loop, 5–7 seconds.
```

## Negative Prompt

```
modern camera, digital display, LEDs, futuristic design, fast motion, shaky cam, cartoon style, exaggerated animation, UI elements, text overlays, studio logos, branding, sci-fi aesthetics
```

## Fallback

If no video is provided, the component will display an elegant CSS animation with:
- Film grain effect
- Vintage vignette
- Animated film reel
- The project's thumbnail as "the subject being filmed"
